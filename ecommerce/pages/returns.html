Returns & Refunds Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2023

At ShopEase, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not entirely happy with your order, we're here to help. This Returns & Refunds Policy outlines our guidelines for returns, exchanges, and refunds.

For the fastest service, you can initiate a return or exchange directly from your account dashboard. Simply go to My Account > Orders > View Order > Return Items.

1. Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, please make sure that:

  • The item was purchased from ShopEase within the last 30 days
  • The item is in its original condition
  • The item is in its original packaging
  • You have the receipt or proof of purchase

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable software products
  • Personal care items (for hygiene reasons)
  • Perishable goods
  • Custom-made or personalized items
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
  • Items that have been used, damaged, or altered after delivery

3. Return Process

3.1 Initiating a Return

To start your return process:

  1. Log in to your ShopEase account
  2. Go to "My Orders" and find the order containing the item(s) you wish to return
  3. Select "Return Items" and follow the prompts
  4. Print the return shipping label (if applicable)
  5. Package the item securely in its original packaging
  6. Attach the return shipping label to the outside of the package
  7. Drop off the package at the designated shipping carrier

If you don't have an account or prefer not to use the online return process, please contact our customer service team at returns@shopease.com or call (123) 456-7890.

3.2 Return Shipping

For standard returns, you are responsible for paying the return shipping costs. If the item is defective, damaged during shipping, or if we sent you the wrong item, we will provide a prepaid return shipping label and reimburse your original shipping costs.

4. Refunds

4.1 Processing Time

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and notify you of the status of your refund. If your return is approved:

  • We will initiate a refund to your original payment method
  • Credit card refunds typically take 5-10 business days to appear on your statement
  • PayPal refunds are usually processed within 2-3 business days
  • Store credit is issued immediately

4.2 Refund Options

You can choose from the following refund options:

  • Refund to original payment method
  • Store credit (with an additional 10% bonus value)
  • Exchange for another item

4.3 Partial Refunds

In some cases, partial refunds may be granted. This typically applies to:

  • Items that are returned with signs of use or wear
  • Items missing parts or accessories
  • Items returned outside the return window but within a reasonable timeframe

5. Exchanges

If you need to exchange an item for a different size, color, or model, please follow the same process as returns. In the return form, select "Exchange" and specify the item you would like instead. If the new item costs more than the original, you will need to pay the difference. If it costs less, we will refund the difference.

6. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. Please provide photos of the damaged item and packaging. We will arrange for a replacement or full refund, including shipping costs.

7. Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund within the timeframe specified above, please check your bank account again, then contact your credit card company or bank, as it may take some time for the refund to be officially posted. If you've done this and still haven't received your refund, please contact our customer service team.

8. Sale Items

Items on sale can be returned unless marked as "Final Sale." Please note that only the amount you paid for the item will be refunded, not the original price.

9. Gift Returns

If the item was marked as a gift when purchased and shipped directly to you, you'll receive a gift credit for the value of your return. Once the returned item is received, a gift certificate will be emailed to you. If the item wasn't marked as a gift when purchased, or the gift giver had the order shipped to themselves to give to you later, we will send the refund to the gift giver.

10. Return Address

Please send all returns to:
ShopEase Returns Department
123 Commerce St, Suite 100
New York, NY 10001

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Returns & Refunds Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: returns@shopease.com
  • Hours: Monday-Friday, 9AM - 8PM EST
