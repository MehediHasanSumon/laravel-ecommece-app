Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our products, services, shipping, returns, and more.

Orders

To place an order, browse our products and add items to your cart. When you're ready to checkout, click on the cart icon in the top right corner and follow the checkout process. You can checkout as a guest or create an account for faster checkout in the future.

You can check the status of your order by logging into your account and going to the "My Orders" section. If you checked out as a guest, you can use the order tracking link provided in your order confirmation email.

You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it by contacting our customer service team. After that, if your order has already been processed for shipping, it cannot be modified or canceled. In this case, you would need to return the item once received.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay. We also offer Buy Now, Pay Later options through Klarna and Afterpay on eligible purchases.

Shipping

Shipping times depend on your location and the shipping method you choose at checkout:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Expedited Shipping: 3-5 business days
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days
  • Same-Day Delivery: Available in select cities for orders placed before 10 AM

Please note that these are estimated delivery times and may vary based on your location and other factors.

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 7-21 business days, depending on the destination and customs processing. Please note that customers are responsible for any duties, taxes, or customs fees imposed by their country.

Once your order ships, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your package by clicking the tracking link in the email, logging into your account and viewing your order history, or contacting our customer service team with your order number.

Yes, we offer free standard shipping on all domestic orders over $50. International orders and orders below $50 will have shipping costs calculated at checkout based on the destination and shipping method selected.

Returns & Refunds

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. To be eligible for a return, your item must be in the same condition that you received it, unworn or unused, with tags, and in its original packaging. For complete details, please see our Returns & Refunds Policy.

To return an item, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your ShopEase account
  2. Go to "My Orders" and find the order containing the item(s) you wish to return
  3. Select "Return Items" and follow the prompts
  4. Print the return shipping label (if applicable)
  5. Package the item securely in its original packaging
  6. Attach the return shipping label to the outside of the package
  7. Drop off the package at the designated shipping carrier

If you don't have an account or prefer not to use the online return process, please contact our customer service team.

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and notify you of the status of your refund. If your return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original payment method. Credit card refunds typically take 5-10 business days to appear on your statement, PayPal refunds are usually processed within 2-3 business days, and store credit is issued immediately.

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. Please provide photos of the damaged item and packaging. We will arrange for a replacement or full refund, including shipping costs. You won't be responsible for return shipping in this case.

Account

To create an account, click on the "Account" icon in the top right corner of the website and select "Register." Fill in your details, including your name, email address, and password. You can also create an account during the checkout process.

If you forgot your password, click on the "Account" icon, select "Login," and then click on the "Forgot Password" link. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

To update your account information, log in to your account and go to the "Account Details" section. Here, you can update your personal information, change your password, manage your addresses, and update your communication preferences.

Yes, we offer a guest checkout option that allows you to make purchases without creating an account. However, creating an account offers several benefits, including faster checkout, order history tracking, wishlist creation, and access to exclusive offers.

Products

Each product page includes a size guide that provides detailed measurements to help you find the right fit. You can access the size guide by clicking on the "Size Guide" link near the size selection options on the product page. If you're still unsure, please contact our customer service team for assistance.

Yes, all products sold on ShopEase are 100% authentic. We source our products directly from manufacturers or authorized distributors to ensure authenticity and quality. We have a zero-tolerance policy for counterfeit items.

Product availability is displayed on each product page. If an item is in stock, you'll see the available quantity or simply "In Stock." If an item is out of stock, it will be marked as "Out of Stock" or "Sold Out." You can sign up for back-in-stock notifications on product pages for items that are currently unavailable.

Many of our products come with manufacturer warranties. Warranty information, if applicable, is listed on the product page under the "Specifications" or "Details" section. For electronics and appliances, we also offer extended warranty options that can be added to your purchase during checkout.
