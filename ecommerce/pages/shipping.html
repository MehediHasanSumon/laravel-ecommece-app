Shipping Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2023

At ShopEase, we strive to provide you with the best shipping experience possible. This Shipping Policy outlines our shipping procedures, delivery timeframes, and other important information to help you understand how we deliver your orders.

1. Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. Orders placed after 12 PM EST will be processed the next business day.

2. Shipping Methods & Delivery Times

We offer several shipping methods to meet your needs:

Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 5-7 business days $4.99 (Free on orders over $50)
Expedited Shipping 3-5 business days $9.99
Express Shipping 1-2 business days $19.99
Same-Day Delivery Same day (order before 10 AM, select cities only) $29.99

Please note that these delivery times are estimates and not guarantees. Delivery times may vary based on your location, customs processing for international orders, and other factors outside our control.

3. Shipping Locations

We currently ship to the following locations:

  • All 50 United States and U.S. territories
  • Canada
  • Most European countries
  • Australia and New Zealand
  • Select Asian countries (Japan, South Korea, Singapore, Hong Kong)

If your country is not listed, please contact our customer service team to inquire about shipping options.

4. Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your package by:

  • Clicking the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
  • Logging into your ShopEase account and viewing your order history
  • Contacting our customer service team with your order number

5. International Shipping

For international orders, please note the following:

  • International delivery times typically range from 7-21 business days, depending on the destination and customs processing.
  • The customer is responsible for all duties, taxes, and customs fees imposed by their country.
  • Some products may not be eligible for international shipping due to regulations or restrictions in certain countries.
  • International orders over $200 may require a signature upon delivery.

6. Shipping Restrictions

Certain products cannot be shipped to specific locations due to local regulations or shipping carrier restrictions. If you place an order for a restricted item, we will notify you and provide options for modifying your order.

7. Order Issues

7.1 Lost Packages

If your tracking information shows that your package was delivered but you haven't received it:

  • Check with neighbors or others at your address who may have accepted the package
  • Look around the delivery location for the package (porch, garage, side door, etc.)
  • Contact the shipping carrier with your tracking number to inquire about the delivery details
  • If you still cannot locate your package, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of the expected delivery date

7.2 Damaged Items

If your item arrives damaged, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damaged item and packaging. We will arrange for a replacement or refund.

8. Shipping for Returns

For information about return shipping, please refer to our Returns & Refunds Policy.

9. Subscription Orders

For subscription-based products, shipping is automatically processed according to your selected delivery frequency. You can manage your subscription shipping preferences in your account settings.

10. Shipping Address Changes

If you need to change your shipping address after placing an order, please contact our customer service team immediately. We can only accommodate address changes if the order has not yet been processed for shipping.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our shipping policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: shipping@shopease.com
  • Hours: Monday-Friday, 9AM - 8PM EST
